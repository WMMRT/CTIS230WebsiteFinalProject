About Us

Webster’s Lawn Maintenance LLC was founded in 2020 by Russell Webster, in Randleman, North Carolina. It was created for a web of reason (hehe). The founder, Russell Webster, was born on July 5th, 1998, and is now a 21-year college senior. The main mission of Webster’s Lawn Maintenance is to keep the lawns/yards of our customer cut, and well taken care off, so that they can have a good-looking yard without having to do the work by themselves. We also provide an avenue for homeowners that do not have the equipment, nor the funds to obtain equipment that is used for lawn upkeep.
